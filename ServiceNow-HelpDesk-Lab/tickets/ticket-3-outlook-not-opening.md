# Ticket 3: Outlook Not Opening

**Incident ID:** INC0010003

## Issue
Outlook crashes immediately after launch.

## User Report
User reports Outlook closes immediately after opening.

## Troubleshooting Steps
1. Restarted Outlook
2. Cleared Outlook cache
3. Ran Microsoft Office repair
4. Restarted workstation

## Resolution
Office repair fixed corrupted application files.

## Root Cause
Corrupted Outlook profile.
